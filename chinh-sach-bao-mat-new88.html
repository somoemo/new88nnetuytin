Thong tin ca nhan cua khach hang

Khi dang ky tai khoan va tham gia ca cuoc tai New88, khach hang se can cung cap mot so thong tin ca nhan nhu ten, dia chi email, so dien thoai, dia chi nha cua va cac thong tin tai khoan ngan hang. Chung toi cam ket su dung thong tin nay chi de thuc hien cac giao dich va lien lac voi khach hang khi can thiet.

Chung toi cam ket khong chia se, ban hoac tiet lo thong tin ca nhan cua khach hang cho bat ky ben thu ba nao tru khi co su dong y cua khach hang hoac theo yeu cau cua phap luat.



Dam bao an toan thong tin

Chung toi luon no luc de bao ve thong tin ca nhan cua khach hang truoc moi hinh thuc tan cong va dam bao an toan tuyet doi cho moi giao dich tai New88. Chung toi su dung cac cong nghe va bien phap bao mat tien tien nhat de dam bao thong tin cua khach hang khong bi ro ri hoac danh cap.

Ngoai ra, chung toi cung yeu cau khach hang tuan thu mot so quy dinh va quy tac khi su dung dich vu cua chung toi nhu khong chia se tai khoan va mat khau voi bat ky ai, dam bao su dung mat khau manh va thay doi thuong xuyen. Chung toi cung khuyen khich khach hang su dung cac cong cu bao mat nhu phan mem chong virus va tuong lua de bao ve thong tin ca nhan cua minh.

Quyen loi cua khach hang

Khach hang co quyen yeu cau truy cap, chinh sua hoac xoa thong tin ca nhan cua minh tai bat ky thoi diem nao. Neu co bat ky thay doi nao ve thong tin ca nhan, khach hang co the lien he voi chung toi de duoc ho tro.

Ngoai ra, khach hang cung co quyen rut lai su dong y cho phep chung toi su dung thong tin ca nhan cua minh bat ky luc nao. Tuy nhien, dieu nay co the anh huong den viec su dung dich vu cua New88.
