Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: Keo 1-1.5

1. Dinh nghia keo 1-1.5

Keo 1-1.5 con duoc goi la keo chau A. Day la mot kieu ca cuoc thuong duoc ap dung trong cac tran dau bong da. Theo do, ti so cua tran dau se duoc cap mot keo 1-1.5 cho doi co the duoc coi la yeu hon. Neu doi nay thua chap 1 ban, tuc la hoa hoac thang, nguoi choi se thang cuoc. Nguoc lai, neu doi nay thua chap 2 ban, nguoi choi se thua cuoc.

2. Loi the cua keo 1-1.5

Keo 1-1.5 mang lai nhieu loi the cho nguoi choi. Dau tien, day la mot keo cuoc an toan hon so voi cac keo khac nhu keo chap, keo ti so hay keo chan le. Voi ti le thang cao, nguoi choi co the dat duoc loi nhuan on dinh trong thoi gian dai. Thu hai, keo 1-1.5 thuong duoc ap dung trong cac tran dau giua doi bong manh va doi bong yeu, dam bao tinh can bang giua hai doi. Dieu nay giup nguoi choi co the de dang danh gia va chon lua doi bong co kha nang thang cao hon.



3. Cach chon keo 1-1.5

De chon duoc keo 1-1.5 hieu qua, nguoi choi can dua ra nhung danh gia chinh xac ve doi bong. Cac yeu to can duoc xem xet bao gom:

  • Phong do hien tai cua doi bong: Neu doi bong dang co phong do cao, co the tu tin chon keo 1-1.5 cho doi nay.
  • Lich su doi dau giua hai doi bong: Neu doi bong da tung thang doi bong khac voi cach biet 1-1.5, co the can nhac chon keo 1-1.5 cho doi nay.
  • Thanh tich thi dau tren san nha va san khach: Neu doi bong co thanh tich tot tren san nha, co the chon keo 1-1.5 cho doi nay neu tran dau dien ra tren san nha cua doi nay.
  • Doi hinh va luc luong cua doi bong: Neu doi bong co day du luc luong va doi hinh manh me, co the tu tin chon keo 1-1.5 cho doi nay.

4. Luu y khi choi keo 1-1.5

Mac du la mot keo cuoc an toan, nhung nguoi choi cung can luu y mot so diem de tranh rui ro va dat duoc ket qua tot:

  • Dung dat qua nhieu tien: Du la keo an toan nhung neu dat qua nhieu tien, ban se rat that vong neu doi bong ban chon bi thua cach biet 2 ban.
  • Dung dat vao doi bong yeu: Du co ti le thang cao, nhung khong nen dat keo 1-1.5 cho doi bong yeu hoac doi bong moi len hang. Nhung doi bong nay co the tao ra bat ngo va lam ban thua cuoc.
  • Can nhac keo chap khac: Neu khong tu tin vao keo 1-1.5, ban co the can nhac chon keo chap voi ti le thang cao hon.
